Financial Aid (Student Loan Trust Fund)

The Students Loan Trust Fund (SLTF) was established in December 2005 under the Trustee Incorporation Act 1962, Act 820.

The objectives of the Trust Fund are to provide financial resou rces for the sound management of the Trust for the benefit of stud ents and to help promote and facilitate the national ideals enshrined in Arti cle 28 and 38 of the 1992 Constitution.
